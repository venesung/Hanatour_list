TRAVEL SHOW 2017

  • Title HanaTour INTERNATIONAL TRAVEL SHOW 2017
  • Period June 9, 2017(Thu.) – June 11, 2017(Sun.) / 3 Days

  • Purpose By offering an opportunity to create a global network in the travel industry, HanaTour will improve the quality across the industry and the services for customers to further develop domestic and overseas tourism industries.

    HanaTour International Travel Show will provide the platform to offer value that satisfies the suppliers, vendors, customers, and stakeholders to help HanaTour become the world's no. 1 business in culture and tourism.
